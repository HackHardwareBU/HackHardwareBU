Tracks

In this year's HackHardware, we are bringing four paths that students can Explore their engineering desires!

Starting Anew

For those who want to take part in a Hackathon, but doesn't know where to start, this track is right for you!

Medical Innovation

Thinking about the next great medical invention? This track is for those who are medicine-focused, those who want to use their engineering skills to explore the biological and biomedical fields

Explore the Cyberspace

This year, we want you to have the opportunity to "Explore the Cyberspace"! In this track, you can do anything that explores the more technical and traditional hackathon ideas. 

Frankenstein 's Tech-ster

Are you interested in innovating already created inventions? In this theme, you will be provided a plethora of inventions that are already created, and your goal will be to combine 2+ devices to create something new!
